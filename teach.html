Methodology

How I turn learning into creation

Phonics and Salad

Challenge

Young learners lacked both language environment and motivation (but they love food!).

Design

I used a multisensory experience to engage students with different interests, created a food-based project to combine interest with learning, and repeated vocabulary across playful scenarios.

Outcome

Students retained vocabulary 2× faster and participated more actively in speaking tasks.
